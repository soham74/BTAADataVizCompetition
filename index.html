The Real Formula

After analyzing 65 fight songs, here's what we learned:

There is no formula.

The most iconic fight songs are the ones that broke all the rules. They didn't say "fight." They didn't use clichés. They were written by passionate students, not hired composers.

Authenticity is the only formula that works.
